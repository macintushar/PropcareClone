About us

Administration

Tulasi Propcare provides rental and property management services for residential property owners in Bangalore and Chennai. Tulasi Propcare is a proprietary business, specializing in residential properties, offering a unique high-touch, high-quality approach to all our services.

Tulasi Propcare was initially started as a service for NRIs to manage their properties in Bangalore and Chennai, Looking at the quality of service provided and convenience, property owners in India have also signed up to have their property managed by us.

Mr. T.M.Selvakumar, proprietor of Tulasi Propcare, has over 20 years of experience in logistics and 5 years in property management. His keen eye for detail, passion to excel and going the extra mile to keep a client happy have won him accolades and a loyal client base.

Ecosystem
